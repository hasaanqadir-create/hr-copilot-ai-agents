## 🛠️ Key Features

The platform includes several specialized workflows to assist your HR team.

**Resume Intake**
The system collects candidate documents and extracts relevant contact information, work history, and skills. This removes the need for manual data entry.

**ATS Scoring**
Each resume receives a score based on how well it fits your job description. The software filters candidates to surface the most relevant profiles.

**Job Matching**
The system identifies the best candidates for specific roles across your database. It compares candidate experiences against the requirements of your open positions.

**Ranking**
After the scoring and matching phases, the agents rank candidates based on their overall qualification levels. This helps you identify top performers quickly.

**Interview Generation**
The software creates interview questions tailored to the candidate's specific background and the job level. You receive a set of prompts to guide your conversations.

**Scheduling**
The application coordinates with candidate calendars to find suitable times for interviews. It manages the back-and-forth communication regarding availability.

**Email & Offer Letters**
The agents draft professional emails regarding screening, interview invites, and offer letters. You can review and edit these drafts before the system sends them.

## 💡 Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues during installation or usage, check these common items:

*   **Blocked Installation:** Windows might show a warning. Click "More Info" and then "Run Anyway" to complete the installation.
*   **Connection Errors:** Ensure your firewall allows the application to access the internet.
*   **API Key Issues:** Check your API key. An expired or incorrect key will prevent the agents from generating responses.
*   **Update Software:** Always check for new versions on the releases page if you notice performance drops.

## 🔒 Privacy and Security

Your data stays secure within your local environment. The application uses your provided API keys to process candidate information. No candidate details reside on external servers outside of your authorized AI provider. You remain in control of which documents the AI processes and which emails you choose to send.
